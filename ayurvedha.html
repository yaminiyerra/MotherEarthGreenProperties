What is Ayurvedic Medicine?

Ayurveda, which translates to "the science of life," is a centuries-old system of natural healing that originated in India. This holistic approach to health and wellness recognizes the interconnectedness of mind, body, and spirit. Ayurvedic medicine seeks to balance the body's energies, known as doshas, to promote optimal health and vitality.
