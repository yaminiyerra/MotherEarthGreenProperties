Welcome to Golden Bamboo Eco Resort, where we invite you to embark on a journey of rejuvenation and bliss. Nestled amidst nature's beauty, our resort offers a myriad of experiences that will rekindle your love for life. Here's a glimpse of what awaits you

Yoga Retreat

Immerse yourself in the serenity of our yoga sessions. Reconnect with your inner self and find balance amid lush green surroundings.

Trekking

Explore the natural wonders that surround us with guided trekking adventures. Discover hidden trails, breathtaking vistas, and the thrill of conquering new heights.

Panchakarma

Indulge in the ancient healing art of Panchakarma, a holistic approach to detoxifying and revitalizing your body. Our experienced practitioners will guide you through this rejuvenating journey.

Fire Camp

Gather around the warmth of a crackling fire under the starlit sky. Share stories, laughter, and create unforgettable memories with your loved ones.

Ayurvedic Medicine

Reconnect with nature's healing wisdom through Ayurvedic treatments tailored to your needs. Experience rejuvenation and vitality like never before.

Organic Food

Savor the flavors of health and sustainability with our delicious organic cuisine. Every meal is a celebration of fresh, locally sourced ingredients that nourish both body and soul.

Indoor & Outdoor Games

Whether you're a fan of board games or prefer outdoor sports, our resort has something for everyone. Engage in friendly competition and make the most of your leisure time.

At Golden Bamboo Eco Resort, we believe in preserving the natural environment while providing you with an unforgettable experience. Join us in celebrating life, nature, and well-being. Book your stay today and let the joy of life unfold before your eyes.
